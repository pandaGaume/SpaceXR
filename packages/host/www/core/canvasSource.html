Canvas Indirection for Tile Textures

Here, we explore the use of canvas indirection to create images that will be used as tile textures. This technique allows us to choose the resolution and display tile images with higher levels of detail. It's particularly useful for 3D terrain rendering, where adapting the resolution of textures is crucial for maintaining performance and visual quality.

The following example demonstrates the use of a 2x2 matrix with a resolution of 512x512 for an LOD offset of 1. This means that with a tile at LOD 10, we will use other tile images at LOD 11. There is no mandatory correlation between resolution and LOD offset, but we are trying to keep the logic used in the tile system where the resolution is multiplied by 2 when the LOD gains 1 unit.
